Mobile SaaS for Agriculture

Run your farm's payroll, expenses and bookkeeping from your phone.

CardinalAgro helps farm owners and agricultural companies track workers, attendance, payroll and receipts — with automatic OCR scanning and a one-tap monthly report ready for the accountant.

Built for agricultural companies

Everything a farm manager needs in one mobile app — offline-friendly, multilingual (Portuguese, Romanian, Spanish, French, English) and designed for use in the field.

W

Workers & nationalities

Register your team with role, job type, nationality and identity documents — searchable pickers built for farm reality.

A

Daily attendance

Log presence, hours and job type per day. Bulk operations for teams working the same plot.

P

Payroll & payslips

Auto-generated payslips with company identity, ready to print or share as PDF.

S

OCR receipt scanning

Scan invoices and fuel receipts with the camera. AI vision extracts the amount, date and supplier automatically.

B

Bookkeeping export

One tap sends the accountant a monthly report and every approved document, each on its own page.

I

Invoicing & billing

Issue invoices, keep a searchable history, upload third-party PDFs — everything in one place.

Where can I get the app?
