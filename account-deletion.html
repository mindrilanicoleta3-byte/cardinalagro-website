Delete your CardinalAgro account

Last updated: 1 June 2026

You can permanently delete your CardinalAgro account and the personal data associated with it at any time. This page is provided in compliance with Google Play's account deletion policy and the GDPR right to erasure.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

  1. Open the CardinalAgro app on your phone.
  2. Sign in with the account you want to delete.
  3. Go to Settings → Company Profile.
  4. Scroll to the "Delete account" section at the bottom of the page.
  5. Tap Delete account and confirm with your password.
Tip. If you are the administrator of a company, deleting your account will also close the entire company workspace. If you only want to remove yourself and keep the company active, transfer ownership first by inviting another admin from Settings → Team.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer sign in to the app (for example, you lost access to the device or the email tied to your account), send us an email from the address linked to your CardinalAgro account:

Email contact@cardinalagro.com with the subject line "Account deletion request".

Please include the following so we can verify your identity and locate your account:

  • The email you use to log in to CardinalAgro.
  • The name of your company inside the app.
  • Confirmation that you want your account and personal data permanently deleted.

We will confirm the deletion by email within 7 business days.

What is deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your login credentials and profile (name, email, hashed password, language and preferences).
  • Company profile data (company name, address, tax ID, logo, accountant email).
  • Worker records, attendance entries and payroll history.
  • Uploaded photos, scanned invoices and receipts.
  • Push-notification tokens tied to your devices.
  • Any personal data we hold that is not subject to a legal retention obligation.

What may be retained (and why)

Some data must be retained for a limited period in order to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce our agreements. This is standard practice and applies to any service you use.

Category Retention after deletion Reason
Invoices, receipts and tax documents 10 years Portuguese tax law and equivalent EU accounting rules.
Payment / billing metadata (last-4, brand, expiry) 10 years Accounting and audit obligations.
Server logs and diagnostics Up to 90 days Security, fraud prevention and troubleshooting.
Encrypted backups Up to 35 days Automatic rolling backups are purged on schedule; we do not restore deleted accounts from backup.
Anonymised, aggregated analytics Indefinite Cannot be tied back to an identifiable individual.
Important. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once we complete the deletion, we will not be able to recover your workers, attendance, payroll or documents. Please download a copy of your data (from Settings → Export data) before deleting your account if you want to keep it.

Timing

Deletion requests submitted through the app are processed immediately. Requests sent by email are processed within 7 business days. All remaining copies in our encrypted backups are automatically purged within 35 days after deletion.

Contact contact@cardinalagro.com and we will help you.
