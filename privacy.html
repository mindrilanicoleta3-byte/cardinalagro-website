Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 June 2026 · Last updated: 1 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how CardinalAgro ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, shares, retains and protects personal data when you use the CardinalAgro mobile application and related services (the "Service").

We are the data controller for the personal data processed in connection with the Service. You can contact us at any time at contact@cardinalagro.com.

1. Data we collect

We collect only the data needed to operate the Service. Depending on how you use CardinalAgro, this may include:

  • Account & company data — name, email, hashed password, company name, address, tax ID, phone number, language and currency preferences.
  • Worker records — names, nationalities, roles, job types, identification numbers, addresses and other data you enter about the workers you employ.
  • Operational data — attendance entries, payroll amounts, vehicles, locations, expenses and invoices you record inside the app.
  • Uploaded documents — photos and PDFs of invoices, receipts and other documents you scan or upload (kept encrypted at rest).
  • Payment data — subscription and billing information processed by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your full card number; only limited metadata (last-4 digits, brand, expiration) is retained for reconciliation.
  • Device & technical data — IP address, device type, operating system, app version, language, crash logs and diagnostic events.
  • Push notification token — an anonymous identifier tied to your device so we can deliver company notifications.
Multi-tenant isolation. Every company account is stored under a strict company_id tenant boundary. Data belonging to one company is never visible to another company on the platform.

2. How we use your data

We use the data described above for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the Service (payroll calculation, expense tracking, bookkeeping export, etc.).
  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To process subscription payments through Stripe.
  • To send transactional emails (invoices, accountant reports, password resets).
  • To deliver push notifications you have opted into.
  • To detect abuse, prevent fraud and comply with legal obligations.
  • To improve the Service, fix bugs and monitor performance (aggregated analytics only).

3. Legal bases (EU users)

Where the GDPR applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract — providing the Service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — securing the platform, preventing fraud, improving the product.
  • Legal obligation — tax, accounting and record-keeping laws.
  • Consent — for optional features such as push notifications and marketing emails. You can withdraw consent at any time.

4. Sub-processors we share data with

We only share data with vetted service providers that help us run CardinalAgro:

Provider Purpose Region
Stripe, Inc. Subscription billing and payment processing. USA / EU
Resend Transactional email delivery (accountant reports, notifications). EU / USA
OpenAI OCR / vision-based recognition of scanned invoices and receipts. USA
Google Firebase (FCM) Push notification delivery to Android devices. USA / EU
Apple Push Notification Service Push notification delivery to iOS devices. USA / EU
Managed cloud hosting provider Application hosting, database storage and backups. EU

We do not sell your personal data and we do not share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

5. International data transfers

Some of our sub-processors are located outside the European Economic Area. When we transfer personal data internationally, we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission and on the additional safeguards provided by each vendor.

6. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described above:

Category Retention period
Active account & company data For as long as your account is active.
Worker, attendance & payroll records For the duration of your account, unless you delete individual entries.
Invoices, receipts and tax documents 10 years, as required by Portuguese tax law and equivalent EU legislation.
Payment / billing metadata 10 years for accounting purposes.
Server logs and diagnostics Up to 90 days.
Backups Up to 35 days after deletion.

When you request account deletion (see the Delete your account page), we delete your account and all personal data that is not subject to a legal retention obligation.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data: TLS in transit, encryption at rest, hashed passwords (bcrypt), tenant isolation on the database layer, least-privilege access controls and regular backups. No system is 100% secure, but we continuously review our practices.

8. Your rights

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom or Switzerland, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten") — see the Delete Account page.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing.
  • Receive your data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (for consent-based processing).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority (e.g., CNPD in Portugal).

To exercise these rights, email us at contact@cardinalagro.com. We answer within 30 days.

9. Children

CardinalAgro is a professional tool for agricultural companies. It is not intended for, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from, children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in the Service, in the law or in our practices. We will publish the new version at https://cardinalagro.com/privacy.html and update the "Last updated" date at the top. Material changes will be communicated inside the app or by email.
